WHO
IS SHE

I write. I code. I dream. I study. I build. I film. I game. I am a woman of breadth, but where does my depth lie?

All the things I want to master probably exceeds my years. I’ve been developing software for the past 6 years and professionally for the past 4 years across industries. As a Computer Science graduate, most of my experience has been as a back end developer implementing algorithms, fulfilling data science needs, building the layout and architectural documents for sustainable android applications, and most recently serving as IT support.

When not developing software, I've invested so much of my time into refining skills across trades and industries. Between sci-fi, east-Asian, and American-Classic film and editing, child developmental psychology and education, writing competitions, computational architecture and computer intelligence, and voice-over, my interests are varied, but united by a common thread of necessitated imagination and perceptiveness in order to produce meaningful results.

As of right now, my current focus is writing and really refining my skills as a Web developer - a role I swore I’d never find fulfilling.

Then I tried it.

I have spent that past 6 months training formally through a Web dev immersive bootcamp and as a contract employee for a solutions-based start-up- and informally by buillding, albeit slowly, a personal app for long and short-term goal management.
